    Eltnum


    Health

    10,400

    Unique Trait

    Eltnum has access to an air grab, as well as having an ammunition system. She can hold 12 bullets at a time, and when reloading, can time it correctly to gain enhanced bullets that grant better frame advantage.

    Vorpal State

    Eltnum's A normals are now whiff cancellable, and she can now backdash cancel any special cancellable moves.
