    Enkidu


    Health

    10,900

    Unique Trait

    Counterhits and whiff punishes cause opponents to enter HAVOC, a state in which they stay in extra hitstun, as well as taking extra damage over time. This allows for unique combo paths.

    Vorpal State

    HAVOC's effects are tripled.(Protip just win Vorpal every cycle 4Head)
