    Yuzuriha


    Health

    10,700

    Unique Trait

    Yuzu has access to a stance system that you maintain by holding down buttons while inputting or after inputting commands for certain moves. Mizuumi has a pretty good breakdown on it, so check it out here.

    Vorpal State

    Force Function has hugely reduced startup both grounded and aerial. Aerial Force Function gets a damage increase as well.

    Combos

    Just like with Seth, Yuzuriha is pretty mechanically difficult to play optimally, So instead of relying on quick reference combos here, I suggest checking out the combo routing on this guide that's linked in the Yuzu bible linked at the top of the page. Also, when you reach the level of understanding with Yuzu that you can learn by watching high level footage, check out Kure's matches online to learn their routes and spacing.
