    Seth


    Health

    9,500

    Unique Trait

    Seth has access to an aerial backdash and a fastfall(j.22, or j.2A+B). Air backdash doesn't consume GRD, and his grounded backdash makes him aerial unless down is inputted before the backdash animation finishes.

    Vorpal State

    Seth's walkspeed increases, and his fastfall distance increases.

    Known Strong Players
  • Oushuu-Hittou
    I'm not linking his twitter to save yall from questionable lewd spam, but he's indubitably the number 1 Active Seth player if we're looking at EVO results. Definitely check his vids on youtube and passing l.ink

    Combos

    Seth is arguably one of the most mechanically difficult characters to play correctly in this game together with Vatista and Yuzuriha, so in this case, instead of looking for cookie cutter combos to learn, I suggest checking out the combo routing in the guide I linked at the top of this page , as well as looking at what oushuu-hittou does if you know his moveset well enough to identify what buttons he's using.
