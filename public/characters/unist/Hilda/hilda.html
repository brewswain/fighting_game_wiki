    Hilda


    Health

    10,100

    Unique Trait

    Hilda's Manifestation buttons' hurtboxes are completely disjointed, thus preventing counterpokes, but aren't treated as projectiles. This causes projectile invincible moves to be wholly ineffective.

    Vorpal State

    Hilda regains meter after using her EX moves, and gains the ability to air teleport after doing a ground teleport.
