    Nanase


    Health

    10,700

    Unique Trait

    Nanase has the ability to jump cancel any of her normals or dash
    attacks on both hit and block by using her Force Function, which she can also use to float and move in the air. This includes her air normals if they're blocked.

    Vorpal State

    Nanase has access to using her ground Force Function after using her ground Force Function
