    Merkava


    Health

    10,400

    Unique Trait

    Merkava has access to the ability to fly. This affects his neutral plan as his air attacks are generally very good.

    Vorpal State

    When Merkava is forced to block, his worms don't vanish. Also, his force function gets increased damage, frame advantage, and is special cancellable on hit.
