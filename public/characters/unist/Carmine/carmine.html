    Carmine


    Health

    10,800

    Unique Trait

    Carmine is able to leave puddles of blood on the stage that can augment his special moves, while draining his own health in recompense. Using certain moves triggers this effect, while costing him 300 health each time. He can regain some health by his grab and by landing his command grab super.

    Vorpal State

    Halves health lost by his puddle triggering moves.
